Hey! This is Aditya Thakkar.

Other than photography & videography, I am very passionate about animal welfare, crazy for desserts, can listen to music all day everyday and love to have fun conversations. I love variety in my life. To keep my creative juices flowing I do multiple activities other than work. Social work is one of the major parts of my life - I founded a community that collars stray dogs with reflective collars to protect them from vehicles in the dark.

What I do

I love telling beautiful stories
through my ‍
videos & photos.

My journey started when I was studying engineering, I realised it wasn't for me. While exploring different paths, I was fortunate enough to intern with a production house. My love for capturing and storytelling grew with time and now I do it for living. Excited to unlock the potential that my camera has.
